About Us

Premier Trekking and Expedition Company

Makalu Mountaineering is trekking and Expedition Company based in Nepal, dedicated to providing high-quality services to adventure seekers from around the world. Our team of experienced and licensed guides are passionate about sharing their love of the Himalayas and providing a safe and memorable trekking and expedition experience.

We offer a variety of trekking itineraries that cater to trekkers of all experience levels. Our treks range from short and easy walks to challenging high-altitude treks that take you through remote villages and stunning landscapes. Our popular trekking routes include the Everest Base Camp, Annapurna Circuit, and the Langtang Valley Trek.

Our team

Our team also offers a range of expedition services that take you to some of the most remote and challenging peaks in the Himalayas. We specialize in organizing expeditions to Mt. Everest, Mt. Makalu, and other 8,000-meter peaks in Nepal. Our experienced team of guides, porters, and support staff ensure that your expedition is safe, enjoyable, and successful.
